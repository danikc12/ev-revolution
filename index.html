Why Choose EVs?

Zero Emissions

EVs produce no tailpipe emissions, reducing air pollution and greenhouse gases.

Lower Running Costs

Electricity is cheaper than gasoline, and EVs have fewer moving parts, cutting maintenance costs.

Quiet & Smooth

EVs offer a silent, seamless driving experience with instant torque.

The Challenges

Range Anxiety

Most EVs have shorter ranges than ICE vehicles, typically 200-300 miles per charge.

Charging Time

Charging an EV takes longer than refueling a gas tank, often 30 minutes to hours.

Upfront Cost

EVs can be more expensive to purchase, though prices are dropping.

EV vs. ICE: The Facts

Energy Efficiency: EVs convert 77% of electrical energy to power, vs. 12-30% for ICEs.

Global Sales: EV sales reached 10 million in 2022, up from 2 million in 2019.

Battery Lifespan: Modern EV batteries last 10-20 years, with warranties up to 8 years.

CO2 Impact: EVs emit 50% less CO2 over their lifetime compared to ICEs, even with dirty grids.
